**Theme configuration rationale:**
- appearance="dark" — activates Radix's dark color scale (gray-1 = darkest, gray-12 = lightest)
- accentColor="grass" — Radix's "grass" scale: grass-9 = #46a758, grass-10 = #3d9a50. Close to our #22c55e brand green. Not identical but consistent family.
- grayColor="mauve" — Mauve grays have a subtle purple warmth (same as radix-ui.com itself). Alternatives: "slate" (blue-tinted), "sage" (green-tinted), "olive", "sand". Mauve is most premium-looking.
- radius="medium" — maps to ~6-8px border-radius on components
- scaling="100%" — default sizing, no adjustments needed

**CSS var coexistence check:**
Radix Themes defines vars at :root AND on [data-is-root-theme="true"] (the Theme element).
Our --mission-control-* vars are on :root (design-tokens.css).
No conflict — different names entirely.

**Panel background note:**
Radix Themes' --color-background defaults to white in light and a dark gray in dark mode.
We're overriding body background in glass-theme.css to #0e0e10 — that's fine, body bg wins.
</context>
