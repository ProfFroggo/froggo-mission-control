### Task 3: Final UI/UX polish pass

Across all social module components:

**Typography:**
- Headers: `text-lg font-semibold text-mission-control-text`
- Sub-headers: `text-sm font-medium text-mission-control-text`
- Body: `text-sm text-mission-control-text`
- Dim text: `text-xs text-mission-control-text-dim`

**Cards:**
- Consistent: `rounded-xl border border-mission-control-border bg-mission-control-surface p-4`
- Larger cards: `rounded-2xl p-6`
- Hover: `hover:bg-mission-control-bg-alt transition-colors`

**Buttons:**
- Primary: `bg-info text-white hover:bg-info/80 rounded-lg px-4 py-2`
- Secondary: `border border-mission-control-border text-mission-control-text hover:bg-mission-control-surface rounded-lg px-4 py-2`
- Danger: `bg-error text-white hover:bg-error/80 rounded-lg px-4 py-2`
- Small: `text-xs px-3 py-1 rounded-lg`

**Empty states:**
- Centered: `flex items-center justify-center min-h-[300px]`
- Icon + title + subtitle pattern
- Lucide icons, no emojis

**Spacing:**
- Card gaps: `gap-3` or `gap-4`
- Section spacing: `space-y-6`
- Padding: `p-4` for content areas, `p-6` for main sections

**No bare borders** — every `border` paired with `border-mission-control-border`
**No hardcoded colors** — all colors from design tokens
